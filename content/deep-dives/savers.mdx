# Savers Vaults and Yield Mechanics

Savers Vaults allow users to earn yield on a single asset without providing the RUNE pair required for traditional liquidity provision.

## Single-Sided Liquidity

In a normal liquidity pool, providing liquidity requires depositing equal value of an external asset *and* RUNE. Savers remove this requirement.

Users can deposit only the external asset (e.g., only BTC or only ETH) and still earn a share of network revenue.

## How Yield Is Generated

Savers earn from two sources:

1. **Swap fees** — A portion of the fees generated by the pool is distributed to Savers.
2. **Block rewards** — A share of the inflationary RUNE emissions flows to Savers.

The yield is variable and depends on overall network activity and the Incentive Pendulum state.

## Trade-offs vs Full Liquidity Provision

| Feature                  | Savers                          | Traditional LP                     |
|--------------------------|----------------------------------|------------------------------------|
| Assets required          | Single asset only               | RUNE + external asset             |
| Impermanent loss         | None                            | Exposed to IL                     |
| Yield                    | Lower on average                | Higher (but more volatile)        |
| Capital efficiency       | Lower                           | Higher                            |
| Management overhead      | Very low                        | Requires active rebalancing       |

## Important Considerations

- Savers still bear smart contract and protocol risk.
- During periods when the Incentive Pendulum heavily favors node operators, Savers yields can drop significantly.
- Withdrawals are always available (no lockups), but large withdrawals can still incur slippage on the underlying pool.

Savers represent THORChain’s attempt to lower the barrier for passive yield while still contributing to the security and liquidity of the network. They are a key part of making the protocol accessible to a broader audience beyond sophisticated liquidity providers.
