# Continuous Liquidity Pools (CLP) Deep Dive

The Continuous Liquidity Pool is the core innovation that allows THORChain to offer native cross-chain swaps without order books or wrapped assets.

## How CLP Works

Every pool consists of RUNE paired with an external asset. The slip-based fee formula protects liquidity providers:

```
Slip Fee = x / (X + x)
```

Where `x` is the input amount and `X` is the current pool depth of that asset.

## Key Properties

- **Always executable**: Trades never fail due to insufficient liquidity.
- **Progressive slippage**: Larger trades pay proportionally more, protecting LPs from manipulation.
- **Single-sided yield via Savers**: Users can earn yield on one asset without providing the RUNE pair.

## Comparison to Traditional AMMs

Unlike constant-product AMMs (Uniswap v2 style), CLP's continuous nature and RUNE as universal settlement asset creates deep unified liquidity across 13+ chains.
